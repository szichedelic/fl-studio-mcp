## Overview

All milestones through v2.1 are complete. The FL Studio MCP Server now supports:
- Foundation & communication (v1.0)
- Note generation with music theory (v1.0)
- Humanization engine (v2.0)
- Plugin control with Serum 2 sound design (v2.0)
- Audio rendering and sample manipulation (v2.0)
- Mixer control with routing and EQ (v2.1)
- Playlist track management and markers (v2.1)
- Project controls: tempo, position, undo/redo (v2.1)
